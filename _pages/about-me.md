Hi, welcome!

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the [Aix-Marseille School of Economics (AMSE)](https://www.amse-aixmarseille.fr/en). My research combines development economics, microeconomic theory and applied microeconomics. I study how climate shocks, social networks, and informal institutions shape household welfare and behaviour in agrarian economies.

My co-advisors are [Habiba Djebbari](https://www.amse-aixmarseille.fr/en/person/habiba-djebbari) and [Timothée Demont](https://www.amse-aixmarseille.fr/en/person/timothee-demont). My primary fields are development economics and applied microeconomics, with a strong interest in climate change, networks, and migration.

**Current visit.** I am currently on a research visit at the [Chair of Development Economics and Economic History](https://www.unifr.ch/edhe/en/) at the University of Fribourg (February–July 2026), hosted by [Christelle Dumas](https://www.unifr.ch/edhe/en/team/christelle-dumas/).
